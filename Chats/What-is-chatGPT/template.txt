ChatGPT Prompt Template
Topic: What is ChatGPT?
Persona (how ChatGPT should act): Friendly teacher
Audience: Non-technical person
Goal/ Purpose: Explain in simple, everyday language so that the reader clearly understands what it does and how they can use it.
Tone/ Style: Clear, friendly, and conversational — like explaining to a curious friend. Avoid jargon or overly technical explanations.
Format/ Structure:
1.	Simple definition (1–2 sentences)
2.	How it works (in easy terms)
3.	Examples of what people can do with it
4.	Short summary or takeaway

Length/ Depth: Short and easy to read — about 3 short paragraphs or under 200 words.
Constraint: No technical terms like “neural networks” or “language model.”
•	Use analogies or real-world examples if helpful.
•	Keep sentences short and natural.

Output Style:
•	Write in Markdown or plain text.
•	Use bullet points for examples.
